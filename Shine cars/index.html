The car is polished in three stages:

  • First: Polished rough: Remove the deep scratches that are formed by the car wash

  • Second: soft polish: to polish the paint to give it a soft and shiny touch of the genus paint

  • Third: Wax layer (Wax) is a protective layer that reduces the proportion of ripples in the paint to help protect against sunlight and oxidation
