In Taipei we have a tour bus, it takes you around the city and famous places.

Taipei Sightseeing gives tourists a different perspective when they travel around Taipei. Bus runs along two routes, both leaving at Taipei Main Station Exit M4. On average, red route takes 75 minutes, blue route takes 120 minutes. Bus intervals are approximately 30-40 minutes. Services provided on the bus include Wi-Fi internet and a tour guide headset in English, Mandarin, Japanese and Korean.
